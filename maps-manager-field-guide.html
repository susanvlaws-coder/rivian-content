1 PRE-SHIFT HUDDLE: Setting the Tone

The Goal: Counteract the "Speed vs. Accuracy" pressure before the line starts. Frame MAPS as a tool for uptime, not just data entry.

The Script (Say this): "Team, our goal today is zero unplanned downtime. We use MAPS to spot issues before they stop the line. If you hit a snag with the tablet, call me immediately. Do not bypass the system to 'save time'â€”accurate data keeps the line running."
2 THE FLOOR WALK: Visual Management
Tablet Proximity Check Is the device mounted at the station? If it's more than 3 steps away, adoption drops by 40%.
The "Paper Note" Hunt Look for operators writing data on scraps of paper to 'type in later.' This causes batch-errors. Stop this behavior immediately.
The 30-Second Rule Observe one entry. If it takes >30 seconds, the operator needs re-training on the UI shortcuts.
